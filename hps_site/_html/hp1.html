HARRY POTTER SERIES

The Philosopher Stone

Harry Potter thinks he is an ordinary boy. He lives with Uncle Vernon, Aunt Petunia, and his cousin, Dudley, who make him sleep in a cupboard under the stairs. Then Harry starts receiving mysterious letters and his life is changed forever. He is whisked away by a beetle-eyed giant of a man and enrolled in Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardy.
