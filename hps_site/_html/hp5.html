HARRY POTTER SERIES

The Order of the Phoenix

Harry Potter is due to start his fifth year at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardy. He is desperate to return to Hogwarts and find out why his friends, Ron and Hermoine, have been so secretive all summer. But before he even gets to school, Harry survives a terrifying encounter with two Dementors, attends a court hearing at the Ministry of Magic and is escorted on a nighttime broomstick ride to the secret headquarters of a mysterious group called Order of the Phoenix...
